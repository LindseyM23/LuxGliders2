About Us

Welcome to Lux Gliders, where luxury meets convenience in the world of beauty. Founded in 2001, Lux Gliders was born out of a passion for delivering high-end beauty services to clients who desire convenience without compromising on quality. Our mission is to bring the salon experience directly to you, wherever you may be.

At Lux Gliders, we specialize in makeup, nail artistry, and wig installations, offering a comprehensive range of beauty services designed to enhance your natural elegance. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing personalized, on-the-go beauty solutions tailored to your unique needs and preferences.
